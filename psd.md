# Product Specification Document (PSD) — Lillsyrran

## 1) Project Overview & Goals
**Project:** Lillsyrran (Alingsås)  
**Primary goals:**  
- Improve local visibility  
- Showcase menu and events  
- Drive dine‑in, takeaway, and future online orders  

## 2) Language
- **Swedish only** (for now)

## 3) Tagline
- **Provided tagline:** “smörrebrad & annat gott”  
- **Logo/menu image indicates:** “smörrebröd & annat gott” (confirm final spelling)

## 4) Required Pages
- Home  
- Menu  
- About  
- Events  
- Contact  

## 5) Core Features
- **Menu** with ~4 small sections  
- **Events** as simple announcements (no ticketing)  
- **Contact**: phone, email, address, opening hours

- Clear CTA buttons (Call, Directions, Menu)

## 12) Step‑by‑Step Implementation Plan
1. **Confirm branding assets**: finalize tagline spelling, logo format, and preferred color palette.  
2. **Finalize site structure**: confirm required pages and top‑level navigation labels (Swedish).  
3. **Prepare content**: collect final text for About, Events (4 small items), Contact details, and opening hours.  
4. **Define menu structure**: confirm section headings and item list/prices from the image.  
5. **Build base layout**: header, footer, navigation, and shared layout components.  
6. **Create page templates**: Home, Menu, About, Events, Contact.  
7. **Add content**: insert menu data, events, and contact info; add logo and images.  
8. **Apply styling**: match the reference site’s clean, warm aesthetic and spacing.  
9. **Responsive pass**: verify layout on mobile, tablet, and desktop.  
10. **Content review**: spell‑check Swedish text and verify prices/times.  
11. **Deploy**: publish to chosen static host and connect domain.  
12. **Handoff**: document how to edit menu/events content for quick updates.
